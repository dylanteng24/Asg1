about us

The Box is founded in 2022 by Amatuer Boxer Dylan Teng, together with his team of professional boxing athelete. Dylan has the skills to compete against the best during the Singapore National Boxing Championship 2022.

The Box is about honour, fitness and courage, virtues that are integral to The Box. We are not only designed for fighter who wants to train with true warrior intentions, but also those that just want to use boxing as a fun way to shed a few pounds and get fit.

We offer a friendly family atmosphere that makes training like training with your friends. The training you receive will be tailored to your needs and goals. It will be addictive and will drive you to get fitter and stronger everyday.

For those that want to compete, our professional training team will work with you to develop your skills and strenghthen your mind. We breed winners.
